Easy

Creamy Garlic Pasta

A silky, restaurant-quality pasta you can make at home in under 30 minutes.

⏱
Prep Time 10 min
🔥
Cook Time 15 min
⏰
Total Time 25 min
👤
Servings 2
🥦
Category Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 200 g spaghetti or fettuccine
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Pinch of chilli flakes
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Bring a large pot of generously salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
2
In a wide skillet over medium heat, melt the butter with olive oil. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1–2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to let it brown.
3
Pour in the heavy cream and stir to combine. Reduce heat to medium-low and let the cream simmer for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens slightly.
4
Stir in the grated Parmesan until melted and the sauce is smooth. Season generously with salt, black pepper, and chilli flakes.
5
Add the drained pasta to the sauce. Toss well to coat, adding a splash of reserved pasta water to reach your desired consistency.
6
Plate immediately, garnish with fresh parsley and extra Parmesan. Serve hot and enjoy!
