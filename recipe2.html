Classic Butter Chicken

Murgh Makhani — tender chicken in a luscious tomato and cream gravy, India's most beloved dish.

⏱
Prep Time20 min
🔥
Cook Time30 min
⏰
Total Time50 min
👤
Servings4
🍗
CategoryNon-Vegetarian

Ingredients

  • 600 g boneless chicken, cubed
  • 1 cup thick yoghurt
  • 2 tsp tandoori masala
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 400 g crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tsp cumin, coriander, garam masala
  • ½ tsp chilli powder
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander to garnish
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Marinate the chicken: Mix yoghurt and tandoori masala together. Add the chicken pieces, coat well, and marinate for at least 30 minutes (or overnight in the fridge for best results).
2
Cook the chicken: In a wide pan over high heat with a drizzle of oil, cook the marinated chicken in batches until lightly charred. Set aside. (You can also grill or broil it.)
3
Make the gravy base: In the same pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add onions and cook until golden — about 8 minutes. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 more minutes.
4
Add crushed tomatoes and all the spices. Stir well and simmer on low heat for 10–12 minutes until the oil starts to separate and the sauce deepens in colour.
5
Add the cooked chicken pieces to the gravy. Pour in the cream, add sugar, and season with salt. Stir gently and simmer for 5 more minutes.
6
Serve hot with naan or steamed basmati rice. Garnish with fresh coriander and a swirl of cream.
