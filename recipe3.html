Easy

Classic Caesar Salad

Crisp romaine, golden croutons, and a bold homemade dressing — the salad that never goes out of style.

⏱
Prep Time10 min
🔥
Cook Time5 min
⏰
Total Time15 min
👤
Servings2
🥦
CategoryVegetarian

Ingredients

  • 1 large romaine lettuce head
  • ½ cup shaved Parmesan
  • 2 slices thick bread (for croutons)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Dressing:
  • 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp Parmesan (grated)
  • Salt & black pepper
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Make the croutons: Cut bread into 2 cm cubes. Toss with olive oil, a pinch of salt, and garlic powder if desired. Toast in a pan over medium heat for 5–6 minutes, tossing often, until golden and crisp. Set aside.
2
Make the dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, minced garlic, and grated Parmesan. Season with salt and pepper. Taste and adjust.
3
Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly. Tear or chop into bite-sized pieces and place in a large salad bowl.
4
Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the lettuce and toss well to coat every leaf. Start with half the dressing and add more to taste.
5
Top with croutons and shaved Parmesan. Add a few extra grinds of black pepper and serve immediately for maximum crunch.
