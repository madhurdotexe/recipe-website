Paneer Tikka

Smoky, golden-charred cottage cheese skewers marinated in a punchy spiced yoghurt — India's favourite starter.

⏱
Prep Time15 min + marinating
🔥
Cook Time20–25 min
⏰
Total Time~40 min
👤
Servings4
🥦
CategoryVegetarian

Ingredients

  • 400 g paneer, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup thick yoghurt
  • 2 tsp tikka masala or tandoori masala
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • ½ tsp turmeric
  • ½ tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 capsicum, 1 onion (cut into chunks)
  • Chaat masala for finishing
  • Mint chutney to serve
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Make the marinade: In a bowl, combine yoghurt, all the spice powders, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, oil, and salt. Whisk until smooth.
2
Add the paneer cubes, capsicum, and onion chunks to the marinade. Toss gently to coat everything well. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes (1–2 hours for best flavour).
3
Thread the marinated paneer and vegetables alternately onto metal or soaked wooden skewers.
4
Cooking options: Grill on a barbecue or a grill pan over high heat for 3–4 minutes per side. Alternatively, broil in an oven at 220 °C for 15–18 minutes, turning halfway.
5
Once the paneer has golden-brown char marks and the edges are slightly crisp, remove from heat. Sprinkle with chaat masala and a squeeze of fresh lemon.
6
Serve immediately with mint-coriander chutney, sliced onion rings, and lemon wedges on the side. Best enjoyed fresh off the grill!
