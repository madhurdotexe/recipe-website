Decadent Chocolate Cake

Moist, rich layers of chocolate sponge smothered in glossy ganache — pure celebration on a plate.

⏱
Prep Time20 min
🔥
Bake Time35–40 min
⏰
Total Time~1 hour
👤
Servings8 slices
🍰
CategoryDessert

Ingredients

  • 1¾ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¾ cup cocoa powder
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1½ tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot coffee or water
  • Ganache:
  • 200 g dark chocolate
  • 200 ml heavy cream
← Back to All Recipes

Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Preheat your oven to 175 °C (350 °F). Grease two 9-inch round baking pans and line them with parchment paper.
2
Dry ingredients: Sift together flour, cocoa, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt into a large bowl. Whisk to combine.
3
Wet ingredients: In another bowl, whisk eggs, buttermilk, oil, and vanilla. Add the wet mixture to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Carefully fold in the hot coffee — the batter will be thin.
4
Divide batter evenly between the two pans. Bake for 35–40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the centre comes out clean. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
5
Make ganache: Heat cream until just simmering, then pour over chopped dark chocolate. Wait 2 minutes, then stir from the centre outward until smooth and glossy. Allow to cool until it reaches a spreadable consistency.
6
Place one cake layer on a plate, spread ganache on top, then add the second layer. Pour remaining ganache over the top, letting it drip down the sides. Decorate with chocolate shavings if desired.
